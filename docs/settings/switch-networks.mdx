# Switch networks

You can switch to different networks by accessing your settings in the Station browser extension. Follow the steps below to switch between Mainnets, Testnets, Terra Classic, and LocalTerra. 

1. Open your Station browser extension and click the gear icon \(âš™\) in the upper right corner of the extension. 

<img
    className="readme-img-sm"
    src="/img/screens/settings/gear.png"
    alt="settings"
/>

2. In the settings menu, click **Network**. 

<img
    className="readme-img-sm"
    src="/img/screens/settings/settings-1.png"
    alt="settings"
/>

3. Select your desired network. 

<img
    className="readme-img-sm"
    src="/img/screens/settings/network-1.png"
    alt="settings"
/>

4. Click the **X** symbol to return to your wallet.
